About Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who currently plays as a forward for Al Nassr and the Portugal national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest football players of all time.

Ronaldo began his professional career with Sporting CP in Portugal before moving to Manchester United in 2003. He won three Premier League titles and the UEFA Champions League with the club before moving to Real Madrid in 2009 for a then-world record transfer fee. At Real Madrid, he won four Champions League titles and four Ballon d'Or awards before moving to Juventus in 2018.

Ronaldo has also had a highly successful international career with the Portugal national team. He has scored over 110 goals for his country and helped them win the UEFA European Championship in 2016.
